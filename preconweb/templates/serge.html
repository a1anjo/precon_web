Introduction

Serge Ferrari, established by Serge Ferrari in 1973, is a prominent French company specializing in flexible composite materials. Recognized globally for its innovation, the company designs, manufactures, and markets composite fabrics renowned for their durability, lightweight properties, and resistance to extreme weather conditions. Emphasizing sustainability, Serge Ferrari utilizes patented Précontraint® technology to produce materials with superior performance and longevity, reducing the need for frequent replacements.

Product Portfolio and Innovation

Serge Ferrari offers a diverse product portfolio catering to various industries, including architecture (tensile structures, façades, solar protection), modular structures, marine (yacht and boat equipment), furniture, and environmental solutions (waste management, water management). Their materials, known for their durability and weather resistance, are enhanced by the patented Précontraint technology, ensuring longevity and reducing maintenance costs. Continual innovation is a hallmark of Serge Ferrari, with a focus on developing new solutions to meet the evolving needs of its global clientele.
